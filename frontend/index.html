About Our Center

Committed to quality healthcare and accurate diagnostics.

Banerjee Diagnostic Foundation and Hospaccx is a modern multispecialty diagnostic and healthcare center dedicated to providing reliable medical services with accuracy, compassion, and professionalism.

Located in Sainthia, Birbhum, our center offers specialist consultations, advanced diagnostic imaging, pathology services, and patient care support in a safe and comfortable environment.

We believe early diagnosis, expert medical advice, and patient trust are the foundation of better healthcare. Our mission is to deliver trusted, accessible, and high-quality healthcare services that improve community well-being.
